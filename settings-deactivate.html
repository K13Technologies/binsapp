Deactivate Account

If you no longer need your account on Bins.ai you can deactivate your account here. Any other seperate Bins.ai company accounts you own will not be affected.

Warning: You will lose all your saved data that is linked with this account.

Note: Don't deactivate your account if you are not selling any grain for an extended period of time. You will receive free upgrades throughout the year that may potentially allow your company to earn more profit during the next harvest season.
